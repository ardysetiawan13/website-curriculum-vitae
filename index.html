This is a simple game that i have ever made for technical test interview.
The instruction was develop simple game tic tac toe with simple artificial intelligence.

How to play?
  • Player = Red Box, AI = Black Box
  • The first mover will be random between player and AI
  • Click on white box to marked as yours then the box color will change to red
  • AI will fight back and will be 1 box filled with black color
  • Once player or AI get 3 boxes with same color (horizontal or vertical or diagonal) the game will reset and the winner will get 1 point
  • Repeat the game until you die (just kidding, i mean until you tired ^_^ )
